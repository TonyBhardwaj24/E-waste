About Us

The e-waste crisis is a rapidly escalating global issue caused by the mass disposal of electronic devices. With technology evolving at a fast pace, millions of smartphones, laptops, and household electronics are discarded every year, leading to environmental pollution and health hazards..

E-waste contains toxic materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium, which contaminate soil, water, and air when improperly disposed of. Additionally, valuable resources such as gold, silver, and copper are lost instead of being recycled.

Impact Created


Environmental Damage

E-waste releases toxic chemicals like lead and mercury, polluting soil, water, and air


Health Hazards

Toxic exposure can cause respiratory diseases, neurological issues, and cancer.


Recycling Benefits

Recycling helps recover valuable materials, reducing the need for mining and production..
